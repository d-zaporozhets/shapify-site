05 May 2014


Shapify's next outing will be at [Maker Faire Bay Area](http://makerfaire.com/) on **May 17 & 18**! We'll be showing everyone there how to make 3D selfies using the Kinect Sensor. 

We'll also be running special offers at the booth, so be sure to come by and get yours!

Find us exhibiting together with [Sculpteo](http://www.sculpteo.com/en/), our mega printing partner, in San Mateo County Event Center located at 2495 South Delaware Street, San Mateo, CA 94403.

Find us in the West Lot Start Up Tent, booth WL2-29!
