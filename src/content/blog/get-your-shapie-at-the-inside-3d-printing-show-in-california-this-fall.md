01 Sep 2014


If you happen to be in California this mid-October, drop by the beautiful town of Santa Clara near San Francisco, where Artec will be holding the U.S. premiere of its new 3D selfie setup, [Shapify Booth](https://shapify.me/), at the largest professional 3D printing event worldwide, [Inside 3D Printing](http://inside3dprinting.com/santa-clara/).

Visit our stand to have your body scanned with Shapify in just a few seconds. Wait three minutes and you’ll see your 3D model on screen. If you like it, you can order it to be 3D printed and you’ll get it in the mail a few days later.

In addition to Shapify, we’ll also be displaying our super-fast professional handheld scanners, [Spider](http://www.artec3d.com/hardware/artec-spider/) and [Eva](http://www.artec3d.com/hardware/artec-eva/), used for scanning small and medium-sized objects in 3D.

When: October 21-23

Where: Booth 213, Santa Clara Convention Center, 5001 Great America Pkwy, Santa Clara, CA 95054
