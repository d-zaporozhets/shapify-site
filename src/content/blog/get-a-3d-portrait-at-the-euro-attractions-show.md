02 Oct 2015


A day in an amusement park is an unforgettable experience for kids and adults alike, and if there’s a Shapify Booth there, the visitors’ best memories can be turned into a very special keepsake. You’ll be able to see first-hand how it works at our stand at the [Euro Attractions Show](http://www.iaapa.org/expos/euro-attractions-show/home) in Gothenburg, Sweden this October.

Come to our 3D scanning point on any of the three days of the show to get scanned in the booth for free in a short 12 seconds and check out your most accurate full-color 3D portrait on a computer screen a few minutes later. Want to have your 3D selfie printed out? No problem. Choose the size and leave us your shipping address. Figurine prices start at $169 for an S-size figurine (6"/15cm). An M-size figurine (7.5"/19cm) will cost you $219 and an L (9"/23cm) is worth $299. Delivery is $35. You can also download your 3D model from the Shapify website for $40.

Getting a 3D portrait is not only a super fun thing to do, but also a great new business idea to capitalize on the customer flow in a theme park, zoo, shopping mall or any other busy venue. If this sounds interesting and you want to know more about how to start making a lucrative business with Shapify, let’s discuss it at the Euro Attractions Show.

Apart from featuring the Shapify Booth, the show will offer unique networking opportunities as 425+ international companies working in the leisure and amusement industry will be showcasing their products and services. That’s in addition to a themed conference, roundtable discussions and workshops.

Where: Stand F122, Svenska Massan, Mässans Gata/Korsvägen, 412 94 Gothenburg, Sweden

When: October 6-8

Book your ticket [here](http://www.iaapa.org/expos/euro-attractions-show/registration).
