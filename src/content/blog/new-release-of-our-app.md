08 Jan 2014 

            ## New release of our app

                We are kick-starting year of 2014 with a new release of the Shapify.me application.

Here's what we have been working on:

**Fixes**

* Fixed application crash during data processing on some notebook models using Windows 8.1
  
* Fixed bug in scanning procedure that increased likelihood of problems when scanning
  
* Fixed bug that caused "No Sound" to appear in order confirmation letters
  
* Fixed lags in interface at startup, scanning hindrances

**Improvements**

* Update mechanism was improved

The new release is available on our site and is free to download.

If you have any comments or feedback, you know where to find us.

Yours truly, the Shapify.me team
