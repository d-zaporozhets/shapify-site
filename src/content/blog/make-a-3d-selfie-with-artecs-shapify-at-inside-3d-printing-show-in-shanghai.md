03 Sep 2014


This fall Artec will showcase its 3D selfie machine, [Shapify Booth](https://shapify.me/), at a major 3D printing event, [Inside 3D Printing](http://inside3dprinting.com/shanghai/),**** in Shanghai, which will feature demonstrations of the latest 3D solutions, conference sessions led by industry experts, and the largest array of international exhibitors to date.

Come to our stand and we’ll scan your body in 3D and just a few minutes later you’ll have a fully processed 3D digital model of yourself which you can have 3D printed!

Also on display will be [Eva](http://www.artec3d.com/3d-scanner/artec-eva/) and [Spider](http://www.artec3d.com/3d-scanner/artec-spider/), the only totally mobile [3D scanners](https://www.artec3d.com/3d-scanner) on the market that scan in high resolution and are safe and easy to use.

When: November 4-5

Where: Booth A03, Shanghai Exhibition Center, Nanjing West Rd, Jing’an, Shanghai, China
