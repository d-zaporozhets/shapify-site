            ## 3D selfie weekend at Northwest Comic Fest

                ![](http://www.artec3d.com/upload/iblock/d41/Pirate%20models.jpg)

Hey, comic book fans! You totally won’t want to miss this awesome festival – the [Northwest Comic](http://www.northwestcomicfest.com/) promises a great weekend packed with meetups with artists, authors, famous cosplayers and wrestlers. That’s in addition to film and music festivals, a costume/performance contest and workshops, where you’ll be able to learn and practice a bunch of skills, from comic strip creation to parkour.

Shapify Booth. You’ll probably be wearing something special for the occasion, so why not make a 3D portrait in that outfit? Bring your friends along, maybe ask a star guest to make a shapie with you – this should make your 3D scanning experience even more fun.

The operators of the Shapify Booth, our good friends [Digital Scan 3D](http://digitalscan3d.com/), will be happy to show you your shapie on a computer screen, print it out and deliver it to your address.

Where: Salem Convention Center (200 Commercial St SE, Salem, OR 97301, United States)   
When: August 15-16

Passes are only available to purchase [online](http://www.northwestcomicfest.com/buy-passes/).
