Your family and friends are sure to be impressed when you show them your selfie in 3D. This fall Artec is offering you a chance to make one at [Euromold](http://www.euromold.com/index.php?id=51&l=1), a leading global fair for moldmaking and tooling, design and application development, in Frankfurt am Main.

Come by our stand to check out [Shapify](https://shapify.me/partner/booth), a rotating booth that will 3D scan your body within just a few seconds. You can then check out your digital 3D model, and if you like it, order it to be printed out in 3D and delivered to your door a few days later.

Also, together with our partner, [Antonius Koester](http://www.innovative-cad-cam-solutions.de/en/), we will be exhibiting Artec’s [portable high-quality 3D scanners](https://www.artec3d.com/3d-scanner) Spider and Eva, which are used to scan smaller objects.

**When:** November 25-28

**Where:** Stand E-05, Hall 11, Messe Frankfurt Venue, Ludwig-Erhard-Anlage 1, 60327, Frankfurt am Main, Germany
