Hey there, folks!

Scanning tip of the day:   
Setting up your Kinect at the right height makes a big difference to the quality of your 3D selfie.

We recommend you check the height for every scanning session. Make sure that the sensor is placed at chest level, no higher than 1.2 meters and then you're good to go! Also, don't forget that the Kinect should be set up horizontally. 

If you see that the final model is missing part of the head or legs, check you've set up your device properly and scan again!
