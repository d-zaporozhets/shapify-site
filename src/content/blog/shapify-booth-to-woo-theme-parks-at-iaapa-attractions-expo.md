06 Nov 2015


![](http://blog.shapify.me/content/images/2015/Nov/Pirates_2.jpg)

The theme park capital of the world, Orlando, FL will be hosting the largest international tradeshow for the amusements and attractions industry, [IAAPA Attractions Expo](http://www.iaapa.org/expos/iaapa-attractions-expo/home), where you’ll be able to learn everything you need to know about running a 3D portrait business with Shapify Booth’s one-click set up. Come to our stand or the Showtime Pictures stand for your detailed, full-color 3D portrait that can be turned into a 3D-printed statuette, an exact mini-copy of you. The Showtime stand will feature our partners [Digital Scan 3D](http://digitalscan3d.com/), who operate Shapify Booths in Portland, OR and Space Center Houston.

Shapies are very special personalized keepsakes that can remind you of life’s happiest days, such as a day spent in a park, zoo or museum with your loved ones. All you need to do to make a shapie is step inside the Shapify Booth, strike a pose and hold still for 12 seconds, letting four 3D scanners make a full circle around you to scan you from all angles. Five minutes later you’ll be shown a preview of your 3D portrait, another 15 minutes – and your printable model file is ready.

Order a print and your figurine will be delivered to your address in a few weeks’ time. Depending on the size, figurine prices range from $169 (S size: 6"/15cm) to $219 (M: 7.5"/19cm) and $299 (L: 9"/23cm). Delivery is $35. You can download your 3D model from the Shapify website for $40.

3D portraits can also be shared on Facebook – every person scanned in the Shapify Booth will receive a free preview of their shapie by email so they can embed it in a Facebook post.

Shapify scanning points can be found around the world, and you, too, can open one to make money selling hundreds of shapies a month. We’ll be happy to help you get started.

Where: Stand #4081 for Artec 3D, stand #1873 for Showtime Pictures, North/South Building, [Orange County Convention Center](http://www.occc.net/), Orlando, FL, United States   
When: November 17-20

Register [here](http://www.iaapa.org/expos/iaapa-attractions-expo/registration-hotel-travel).
