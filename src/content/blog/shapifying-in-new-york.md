14 Apr 2015


This is a mega expo week for Shapify – started in [Dubai](https://www.facebook.com/shapify.me/posts/1643213392574954), it continues in New York, at [Inside 3D Printing](http://inside3dprinting.com/new-york/2015/) conference & show. Anyone dropping by our stand (#409) at Manhattan’s Javits Convention Center Wednesday to Friday will have a chance to make a 3D portrait in the Shapify Booth for free and find out everything about Shapify as a great business project to start.

Shapify is a 3D photo booth of the future that takes just a few minutes to produce a person’s image in 3D. Step inside the booth and freeze for 12 seconds – the Shapify automatic 3D scanning technology will do the rest. Your 3D model will be ready within five minutes, and we guarantee you’ll be amazed to see how accurate it will be! If you want to keep your model as a file on your computer, you’ll be able to download it from our website for $40. We can also print out your figurine from the model and deliver it to your address. An S-size figurine (6"/15cm) is $169, size M (7.5"/19cm) comes at $219 and L (9"/23cm) at $299. Delivery is $35.

Besides the 3D selfie opportunity, the New York event is just the right place to be if you want to keep up with the latest trends in 3D scanning and printing. The Inside 3D Printing conference will bring together hundreds of industry visionaries, manufacturers and investors to discuss the challenges of printing just about anything, from food products to human organs. Sounds exciting? Register for the [show](http://inside3dprinting.com/new-york/2015/register/)! Hope to meet you at the Javits Center this week!
