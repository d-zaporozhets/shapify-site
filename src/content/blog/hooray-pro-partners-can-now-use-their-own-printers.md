15 Apr 2014


Good news for Shapify Pro partners! We've added a new feature to the Shapify site so Pros now have the option to download their 3D scans and print their Shapify figurines with their own 3D printers! 

To download one scan costs $20. Discounts are available for downloading 5+ scans. All details can be found on the [Shapify Pro page](https://shapify.me/info/pro).

Watch this space for more exciting developments!

Your Shapify Team
