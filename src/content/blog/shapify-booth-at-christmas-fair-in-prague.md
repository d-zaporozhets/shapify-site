29 Nov 2014


![](http://www.artec3d.com/upload/iblock/aa7/Prague.jpg)

Artec and [Haferrest-Praha s.r.o.](http://3d-figures.eu/) are bringing the Shapify Booth to Prague’s biggest [Christmas fair](http://www.stribrnevanocnidny.cz/2014/cz/intercept.asp) this December.

The 6th annual Fair of Gifts, Decorations and Household Goods will bring together over 350 exhibitors from across Europe. The exhibition grounds will be divided into the fashion zone, a craft fair, a gourmet market and children’s play area. Visitors are welcome to take part in contests, attend performances by drama schools and studios, and choose the most beautiful Christmas tree.

Come to our stand to make a really nice and special Christmas present for yourself or your family – a 3D selfie. Shapify will scan your body in just 12 seconds then take another 15 minutes to produce your ready-to-print 3D model. If you like it, we’ll print it out for you. Three to four days later you’ll be able to pick up your figurine at the Haferrest-Praha s.r.o. office (Na Příkopě 23, Panská pasáž, 3D STUDIO, Praha 1, 110 00).

Prices per figurine range from 2,200 CZK for a 12cm-tall figurine, 2,800 CZK (15 cm), 3,950 CZK (18 cm), 4,950 CZK (20 cm) and 6,950 CZK (25 cm).

Where: #2B47, Hall 2, PVA EXPO PRAGUE (Beranových 667, Praha 9 – Letňany)

When: December 11-14, 10 a.m. – 6 p.m.

Admissions: 40 CZK; family (2 adults + 2 children) – 80 CZK; children under 6 years old – free; [online registration](http://www.stribrnevanocnidny.cz/2014/cz/vstupenka.asp), students, pensioners, disabled – 20 CZK; parking – free
