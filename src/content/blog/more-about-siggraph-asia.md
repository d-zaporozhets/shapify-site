23 Nov 2013


Siggraph Asia has officially closed its doors and we can't wait to share the results. For three days we held onsite presentations of Shapify.me in Hong Kong to a most enthusiatic and wonderfully proactive audience. Everyone wanted to learn more about the magic of Shapify.me. We received tons of feedback which we'll be taking into consideration for future releases of our app.
