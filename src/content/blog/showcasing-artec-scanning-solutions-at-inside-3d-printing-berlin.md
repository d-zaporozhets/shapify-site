02 Mar 2015


![](http://www.artec3d.com/upload/iblock/d75/Inside%203D%20Printing.jpg)

Artec is bringing its best-selling handheld scanners, Eva and Spider, along with a rapid body scanning solution, the Shapify Booth, to the [Inside 3D Printing](http://inside3dprinting.de/) show, to be held in Berlin.

Eva and Spider capture the surface of a scanned object in very fine detail, delivering accurate scans in brilliant color, and Shapify, an innovative setup employing four wide-view scanners fixed to a rotating rig, takes just 12 seconds to scan your body and then 15 minutes to produce a ready-to-print 3D model. If you like, we can print out your 3D model and deliver it to your address. Prices per figurine start from $169 for an S-size figurine (6"/15cm), $219 for an M-size figurine (7.5"/19cm) and $299 for an L-size figurine (9"/23cm). Delivery is free.

Visitors will be offered a chance to not only see and try out state-of-the-art products but also discuss the latest trends in the industry with leading experts. The Inside 3D Printing Berlin agenda features sessions on the application of additive manufacturing technologies in the automotive and aerospace industries, bionics and other fields. On Day 1, Artec CBDO, Leo Volkov, will be speaking on [using 3D scanning technologies to streamline businesses](http://inside3dprinting.de/berlin2015/agenda/#p1644).

Click [here](http://inside3dprinting.de/register/) to register online and get a discount on admission.   
Where: Booth 320, Estrel Berlin Hotel & Convention Center (Sonnenallee 225)   
When: March 3-4
